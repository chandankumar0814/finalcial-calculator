What is EMI?

An Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) is the fixed payment made every month towards repaying a loan. Early EMIs are interest-heavy; as the loan progresses, a larger fraction goes towards the principal (reducing balance method).

What is Position Sizing?

Position sizing is the process of determining how many units of a security to buy, based on your risk tolerance and account size. It is the most important part of risk management.

Risk Warning

Never risk more than 1-2% of your capital on a single trade. Position sizing is crucial for long-term survival in markets.
